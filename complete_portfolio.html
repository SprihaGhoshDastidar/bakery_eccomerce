About Me

(Kakoli Sengupta: Baker)

HMH is a kolkata based mom's bakery situated in Sector-2, Salt-Lake. Each cake is freshly baked by Kakoli Sengupta. Our motive is to spread happiness through flour, sugar, butter, and lots of love.

We are one of the top 20 home bakeries in Kolkata. Both COD and Delivery are available.
